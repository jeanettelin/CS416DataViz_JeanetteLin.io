Ratio of Male vs Female Summer Olympians Throughout History

Post World War 2 Olympics

After the pause we see in World War 2, we can see the ratio of female athletes increase in the Olympic Games. The female ratio was increasing year after year, and in 1976, we see that females made up 25% of the total athlete pool. By 2016, females had made up almost half of the Olympians. Overall, through this time series, we can see that changing societal norms have allowed more and more female participation in sports.
