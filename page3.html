Ratio of Male vs Female Summer Olympians Through History

Ratio by Country

In this page, we can explore the gender ratio by each country. In the "Add Country Trend", we can add a country and see how their ratio of females trend throughout the years. Most countries follow the general trend of having their male and female lines heading towards the middle ground line of 50%, while some countries see large fluctuations from year to year.

For example, we if we look at USA, we can see the percentages are very close to the overall trend, and we also see that the number of females had exceed males for USA in 2016. If we look at CHN (China), we see that after not having participated in the Olympics for several decades due to political reasons, when they did rejoin, their ratio of females jumped up.
